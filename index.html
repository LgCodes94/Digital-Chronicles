About me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Luis Garcia, and I'm thrilled to share my journey with you. While my career initially took me down the path of banking and sales for the past 10 years, I recently embarked on an exciting new adventure - a career in programming.


My passion for programming ignited when I discovered the boundless creativity and problem-solving opportunities it offers. The ability to build something from scratch and see it come to life in the digital realm has become my driving force. It's the kind of challenge that keeps me awake at night with excitement and curiosity, and I can't wait to explore the endless possibilities this field has to offer.


In this portfolio, you'll find a collection of projects that showcase my dedication to learning and growing in the programming world. From web development to software applications, I've been honing my skills and applying my newfound knowledge to create practical and innovative solutions. Each project represents a milestone in my journey, and I can't wait to see where this path takes me next.


Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I've enjoyed creating it. If you're interested in collaboration or have any questions, please don't hesitate to reach out. Let's connect and make the digital world a better place, one line of code at a time!
